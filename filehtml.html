Our Services

node is a bespoke design service that creates the best interior planting composition for your space.
We offer interior landscape design for homes, workplaces and commercial purposes.
House
Node is committed to turn your house or flat into a ‘green home’. We suggest you the most suitable plants for each room and show you how to care for them. We have various plant packages from beginner pack to the green thumb selection.
Workplace
Plants are the best and easiest tool to bring creativity and productivity to a workplace. Node helps you to create the perfect environment for your office providing a bespoke interior landscape design right for your space.
Commercial
Any space with beautiful green leaves becomes more attractive for your customers. Node provides planting design for commercial interiors such as restaurants, stores and coffee shops to create a welcoming atmosphere for the customers.
